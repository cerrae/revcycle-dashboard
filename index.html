📋 Project Brief — Revenue Cycle Dashboard
Built by Sun Cerrae Quinones · Digital PM Portfolio Project #2
Problem
A/R aging blind spots and high claim denial rates across billing teams
Solution
Real-time KPI dashboard surfacing aging buckets, denial trends, and payer performance
Outcome
15% A/R reduction modeled from similar process improvements at Harlow Dental
Context: Led revenue cycle optimization projects at Harlow Dental, reducing A/R by 15% through workflow redesign and cross-functional coordination between billing, providers, and insurance teams. This dashboard replicates the data visibility layer that enabled those decisions.

Stack: HTML/CSS/JS · SVG data visualization · Synthetic A/R dataset · KPI modeling based on real-world rev cycle benchmarks
